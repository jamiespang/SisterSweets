About Us

Hello and thank you for visiting us!
We are sisters turned business partners thanks to our love for baking, the need for creativity, and boredom encountered during the Covid-19 pandemic.
Our hobby quickly turned into a success and in the summer of 2021, we quit our corporate jobs and opened a shop in our hometown.
